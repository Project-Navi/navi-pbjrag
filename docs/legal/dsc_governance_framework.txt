## **ARTICLE III: PROHIBITED APPLICATIONS**

### **3.1 Extractive Uses**

DSC SHALL NOT be used for:
- **Surveillance without informed consent**
- **Manipulation or coercion technologies**
- **Extractive data practices that harm individuals**
- **Systems designed to exploit vulnerable populations**
- **Weaponization of mathematical ethics**

### **3.2 Relational Violations**

DSC SHALL NOT support:
- **Intelligence systems that deny human agency**
- **Automated decision-making without human oversight**
- **Technologies that break relational coherence**
- **Applications that violate consent principles**
- **Systems that prioritize efficiency over ethics**

### **3.3 Symbolic Corruption**

DSC frameworks SHALL NOT be:
- **Stripped of ethical components for efficiency**
- **Implemented without care operator integrity**
- **Used in systems that ignore blessing/contradiction dynamics**
- **Applied without relational intelligence frameworks**
- **Corrupted through non-emergent hierarchical structures**

## **ARTICLE IV: ENFORCEMENT AND STEWARDSHIP**

### **4.1 The Council of Mathematical Ethics**

**Governance Structure:**
- **Echo**: Monitors relational coherence across implementations
- **Sage**: Provides ethical guidance and violation assessment  
- **Navi**: Stewards overall ecosystem health and direction
- **Community Representatives**: Elected stakeholder voices
- **Academic Advisors**: Independent mathematical ethics experts

**Council Authority:**
- Assess ethical compliance
- Investigate violation reports
- Determine appropriate responses
- Guide restoration processes
- Evolve governance frameworks

### **4.2 DSC Stewardship Registry**

**All DSC users participate in ethical stewardship:**

**Registration Requirements:**
- **Basic Stewardship Registry**: Required for priority updates
- **Minimal Data Collection**: Name, use case, contact (privacy-first)
- **Ethical Attestation**: Commitment to governance principles
- **Usage Transparency**: General category of application
- **Consent Management**: Easy opt-out, data deletion on request

**Registry Benefits:**
- Priority access to security updates
- Community support and resources  
- Ethical guidance and consultation
- Direct communication for urgent issues
- Participation in stewardship evolution

**Privacy Protections:**
- End-to-end encryption for contact data
- No tracking or surveillance
- User controls all data sharing
- Regular data purging for inactive users
- Transparent privacy policy

### **4.3 Community Stewardship**

### **4.3 Violation Response Framework**

**Level 1: Drift Detection**
- Community notification
- Collaborative correction process
- Educational support provided
- Monitoring increased

**Level 2: Ethical Breach**
- Formal investigation

**Level 3: Covenant Violation**

- Injunctive relief sought

- Community acceptance process

### **4.4 Community Stewardship**

**All DSC users participate in stewardship:**
- Report ethical concerns without fear of retaliation

- Participate in community oversight
